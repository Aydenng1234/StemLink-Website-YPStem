About Youth Pioneers in STEM

Youth Pioneers in STEM (YPStem) is a nonprofit organization with the goal of educating future leaders in society and helping students discover their passion in all fields of STEM via workshops, competitions, and other initiatives! We seek to spark passion within future leaders of society, and our organization truly advocates for accepting students from all backgrounds under one common interest. Stemlink is a YPStem initiative seeking to connect students with others who have similar interests and provide them with opportunities in STEM.
